Design Process

Research

To begin the design process, I conducted extensive research into the needs and preferences of our target audience. This included conducting user interviews, reviewing industry research, and analyzing competitor apps.

Competetive Analysis

To build a strong and sturdy base for Serenity, I had to go on an adventure and check out what the cool meditation apps were up to. I wanted to see what they were missing out on, you know? So, I took a look at what users really wanted and picked out the most important features. And guess what? Serenity can totally take advantage of these features and leave other apps in the dust! Turns out, none of the big competitors were helping users find enough time for meditation or keeping them consistent. Talk about a missed opportunity!

Style Guide

Serenity's branding is all about chill vibes, so we went with soothing colors and picked Poppins as the main typeface. The green hue is like a virtual hug, making users feel at ease. And Poppins? It's like a chameleon, transforming from thin to thick with ease. It's the perfect match for our app.
